Here are some of my   projects:

  • "Flutter" Butterfly Scene

    Unreal Engine, Maya, NukeX

    During my internship at Academy Gold, I worked as a VFX artist on a short film where our team integrated a CG butterfly into live-action footage. Over the course of 8 days, we completed 30 VFX shots, focusing on realistic lighting, motion tracking, and seamless compositing to bring the butterfly to life within the scene. This project allowed me to collaborate closely with the production team, applying my skills in visual effects to enhance the storytelling through detailed and dynamic VFX work.

  • Beyond Earth

    Unreal Engine, Maya, NukeX, Photoshop, Syntheyes

    For my personal project Beyond Earth, I created an unknown CG environment and integrated it with green screen footage. I built the entire environment in Unreal Engine and composited it using Nuke to blend the virtual world seamlessly with the live-action footage. This project allowed me to explore combining real-time 3D creation with post-production techniques to create a visually immersive scene.

  • Spider Flesh Monster Scene

    Unreal Engine, Maya, NukeX

    I added a CG spider monster into live-action footage. I used Maya to place and light the creature, rendered it in Unreal Engine, and finished the shot in Nuke. It was a great learning experience in combining CG with real footage.

  • Dino Beast

    Unreal Engine, Maya, NukeX

    I worked with my friend Wills Groth on his monster animation. I built the entire environment, rendered the scenes, and did the compositing. It was a fun collaboration that allowed us to explore our creative ideas.
